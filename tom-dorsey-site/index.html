    About Tom Dorsey

    Tom Dorsey is a prominent figure in the financial industry, renowned for his expertise in technical analysis and investment strategies. In 1987, he co-founded Dorsey, Wright & Associates, a registered investment advisory firm specializing in equity analysis and options strategies. The firm gained recognition for its focus on Point & Figure charting, a method used to forecast and track market prices. In 2015, Nasdaq acquired Dorsey, Wright & Associates, integrating its services into Nasdaq's offerings. NASDAQ

    Throughout his career, Dorsey has authored several influential books, including "Point & Figure Charting: The Essential Application for Forecasting and Tracking Market Prices" and "Tom Dorsey's Trading Tips: A Playbook for Stock Market Success." These works provide insights into his methodologies and have been valuable resources for traders and investors. AMAZON

    Dorsey is also recognized for his contributions to financial media, frequently appearing on programs such as Fox's "Cavuto on Business" and "Bulls & Bears." He is a sought-after speaker on topics related to the stock market, technical analysis, and relative strength investing. CMT ASSOCIATION
